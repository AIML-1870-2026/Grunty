How DrugSafe Works

1

Name Resolution

Your drug names are sent to RxNorm (NIH) to resolve typos, brand/generic mismatches, and return canonical RxCUI identifiers.

2

Interaction Check

DDinter 2.0 (302,516 DDI records across 2,310 drugs) provides the primary severity rating and mechanism of action.

3

Parallel Enrichment

openFDA drug labels, FAERS adverse event reports (real-world data), and recall status all load simultaneously.
